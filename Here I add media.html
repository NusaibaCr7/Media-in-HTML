VIQARUNNISA NOON SCHOOL AND COLLAGE

Viqarunnisa Noon School and College (VNSC) is a premier educational institution in Bangladesh, located in the heart of Dhaka. Founded in 1952, it was named after Begum Viqar un Nisa Noon, the wife of Feroz Khan Noon, a former Governor-General of Pakistan, in recognition of her contributions to education and welfare.

History and Legacy:
VNSC began as a modest initiative but quickly evolved into one of the most esteemed educational establishments in the country. Its rich history is intertwined with a commitment to fostering academic excellence and personal growth among its students.


Academic Excellence:
The institution is renowned for its high academic standards. It offers education from primary to higher secondary levels, adhering to the Bangladeshi National Curriculum. The school is known for its consistent stellar performance in national board examinations and competitive university admissions.


Campus and Facilities:
Viqarunnisa Noon School and College operates across four campuses in Dhaka:
  1. Main Campus: Located in Bailey Road, it is the hub of most activities.
  2. Azimpur Branch
  3. Bashundhara Branch
  4. Dhanmondi Branch
Each campus is equipped with modern facilities, including well-furnished classrooms, libraries, science laboratories, and sports grounds, creating a conducive environment for learning.


Extracurricular Activities:
VNSC places significant emphasis on extracurricular engagement to ensure holistic development. Students actively participate in:
  • Debates and quiz competitions



  • Cultural programs

  • Science fairs and exhibitions




  • Sports tournaments


These activities help students cultivate leadership skills, creativity, and teamwork.

Faculties:
The institution boasts a team of dedicated and highly skilled teachers who are committed to nurturing academic and moral excellence in students. Their guidance and mentorship have been instrumental in shaping the future of countless young women.



Empowering Young Women:
As an all-girls institution, Viqarunnisa Noon School and College has played a pivotal role in empowering women through education. It fosters confidence, discipline, and resilience, preparing students to excel in various fields.



Conclusion:

Viqarunnisa Noon School and College stands as a symbol of excellence in education in Bangladesh. With its unwavering commitment to nurturing academic brilliance and character development, it continues to inspire and empower generations of young women, shaping a brighter future for the nation
